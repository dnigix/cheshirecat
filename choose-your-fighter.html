Choose your fighter

April 26, 2025

A lot of these overlap in terms of general principles, but I feel like choosing between these options (or at least figuring out where you sit between them) gives a good aggregate of your moral values.

  • Enlightenment (Steven Pinker, Paul Graham, Patrick Collison) or Reaction (Peter Thiel, Curtis Yarvin, Nick Land, Andreesen)?
  • Matisse (substance) or Picasso (form)?
  • Tolstoy, Pushkin, Bunin (West) or Dostoyevsky, Yesenin (East)?
  • Doomer or e/acc?
  • Einstein, Oppenheimer (dove) or Teller, Von Neumann (hawk)?
  • Nabokov, Amis, Larkin, Aristotle, Steve Jobs (aesthetics as a virtue) or Dostoyevsky, Mann, Camus, Mayakovsky (substance as a virtue)?
  • Mandelstam the dreamer or Pasternak the visionary?
  • Mishima (violent beauty) or Kawabata (silent beauty)?
  • Akhmatova or Tsvetaeva?
  • God or Nietzsche?
  • Feynman, Dyson (intuition) or Von Neumann, Grothendieck (rigor)?
  • Tchaikovsky, Ravel, Rachmaninov (substance) or Bach (form)?
  • Skinner or Chomsky?
  • Byzantine Christianity or North European Christianity?
  • OpenAI (builders) or DeepMind (scientists)?
  • Fence or anti-Fence?
  • Zero-sum or non-zero sum?
  • Red or White?
  • Haussmann or Victoria?
  • Bostrom, Strugatsky or Banks, Asimov?
  • Lisp or Python?
  • Silicon Valley or Ivory Tower?
  • Eco or Borges?
  • Stripe Press, Works In Progress, IFP or Palantir?
  • Do what you love, and you will become good, or do what you're good at, and you will come to love it?
  • Barbican or Fallingwater?
  • Wallace or Hemingway?
  • Catherine, Peter, Ataturk, Meiji or Tokugawa, Nicholas I?
